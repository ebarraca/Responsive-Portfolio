About Me

I am a Chicago-based Art Director/Graphic Designer with 15 years experience in print design in both Marketing and Publishing. I am currently enrolled in Northwestern's Web Coding and Development Boot Camp with the hopes to make a transition to a Web Developer next year. I also have a photography business and have been fortunate to document weddings and families for the last 8 years. My work can be found at ericabarracaphotography.com.

I live in Morton Grove with my son, Milo and our two pitbulls, Maddox and Stella. I love to travel, try all the new vegan food in Chicago, volunteer at our local animal shelter, and see live music whenever possible.
